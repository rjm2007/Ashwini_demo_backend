src/main.py
Starts FastAPI and triggers background SQS consumption.

src/config.py
Loads environment variables into one settings object.

src/database.py
Defines shared SQLAlchemy connection.

src/workers/sqs_consumer.py
Reads messages from SQS queue and routes each to the pipeline orchestrator.

src/workers/pipeline_orchestrator.py
Runs OCR, metadata extraction, chunking, embeddings, vector upsert, and status updates.

src/services/*
Contains wrappers for S3, Textract, LLM, embeddings, chunking, and Qdrant.

src/query/*
Contains query-time intent classification, certified-only retrieval, reasoning, and confidence scoring.

src/api/routes.py
Exposes health and internal processing/query endpoints.

src/prompts/*
Stores extraction and query prompts with strict anti-injection rules.
